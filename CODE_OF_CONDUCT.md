# Code of Conduct

## Summary
This project is committed to a respectful, professional, and collaborative
environment. Be considerate, communicate clearly, and focus on constructive
outcomes.

## Expectations
- Treat everyone with respect and courtesy.
- Assume good intent and engage in good-faith discussion.
- Avoid harassment, hate speech, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Keep discussions on-topic; avoid unrelated debates (e.g., politics, religion,
  race, gender, or other personal attributes).
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

## Scope
This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests,
discussions, commits, and any other community interaction tied to this repo.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content or restrict access as needed to maintain a
healthy project. If you experience or witness conduct that violates these
guidelines, please open an issue or contact the repo owner.
